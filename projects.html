Unnamed Board Game

Note: This is in the very early stages of planning.
I am developing a board game about cracking - set in a cyberpunk universe the players assemble attack programs to steal valuables from their targets. Unlike most games of this style, the players have no ability to control the programs once they unleash them - cyberspace is too fast for humans to respond in the way that people do in most cyberpunk settings!

For extra nerd cred, I plan to write the rulebook in C++/Markdown.
